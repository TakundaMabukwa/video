‚ö†Ô∏è Why You're Not Seeing Video

Your cameras are sending raw H.264 video frames. Browsers cannot display H.264 frames directly - they need to be:

  • Decoded - Convert H.264 to RGB pixels (requires decoder)
  • Transcoded - Convert to browser-friendly format (HLS, WebRTC)
  • Rendered - Display on canvas or video element

What you're seeing now is frame metadata (count, type, size) - which proves the video data is arriving correctly.

Option 2: FFmpeg Transcoding (Best Quality)

Convert H.264 to MP4 for browser playback:

ffmpeg -i recording.h264 -c:v copy output.mp4

Then play the MP4 file in any browser.

Option 3: Live HLS Streaming (Real-time)

Requires FFmpeg to be installed and running on the server. This converts frames to HLS format in real-time.
